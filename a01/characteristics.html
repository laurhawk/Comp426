Characteristics of Vampires

Okay, you've learned the history of vampires. Now, how are you supposed to recognize one in public?

Look out for:

  • pointy canine teeth
  • drinks blood
  • immortal
  • avoids sunlight (maybe even sparkles?)
  • repelled by garlic
  • heightened senses
  • pale or matte skin
  • cannot see reflection in mirror or shadow
  • conventionally attractive or hideously ugly, no inbetween
  • does not age
  • sleeps in coffins or does not sleep
  • red eyes
  • avoids crucifixes
  • cold
